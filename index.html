Why Spaced Out?

Mobile first
Targeting the ever-growing mobile users


Marketing your business can be hard and it can definitely get harder if you don't target the right users. We, at Spaced Out, take a mobile first approach with everything we do - SEO, ad campaigns, content marketing, and web user experience design.


Quick stat: As of January 2018, almost 80% of the Internet users in India browse on mobile.
Source

User experience first
Responsive design that works well on different screen sizes.


User experience is at the nexus of what we do to acheive our goals, and yours too. We don't believe in unrefined experiences that are either too barebones or too novel. Focusing on users coming online for the first time is a need of the decade. We do exactly that.


Quick stat: 53% of mobile site visitors will leave a site if it takes more than three seconds to load.
Source

Holistic marketing
Marketing to people at the right place and the right time.


Marketing to people on the Internet is not one-size-fits-all. Depending on your marketing goals, various audiences need to be targeted at different points in their online journey through different channels. We know that digital marketing is a holistic approach that needs to be seen like a jigsaw puzzle.


Quick stat: 75% of smartphone owners turn to a search engine first to address their immediate needs.
Source

Data-driven
Understanding your audience through data.


As we have seen in the past few years, data is the new oil. With new data constantly being created from various devices, it is essential for a digital marketer to utilise the available information to understand users and their behaviour. Responsibly using tools like Google Analytics, AdWords, Moz, Google Trends, and social media ad platforms, we analyse your brand's audience and help you make informed decisions by not depending too much on either data or intuition, but a blend of both.


Quick stat: Nearly 90% of marketers say that understanding user journeys across all channels and devices is critical to their success.
Source
